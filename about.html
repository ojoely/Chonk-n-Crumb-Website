About Chonk n’ Crumb 🍪

Established in August 2025, Chonk n’ Crumb began with one mission: to create cookies that deliver the perfect bite every time.

Since then, we've proudly served over 400+ cookies — and we’re nowhere near stopping. Our fresh, chonky, crumbly cookies are crisp on the outside, soft on the inside, and made to bring a little happiness with every bite.

Now, we’re growing into cakes, carrying the same love, flavor, and joy into every slice.

More cookies. More cakes. More smiles.
